Biography

Hi, I am Hao Shen! I am currently a Lecturer at Anhui University of Science and Technology (AUST). Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. degree 😀😀😀🎉🎉🎉 from the School of Computer Science and Information Engineering, Hefei University of Technology (HFUT), supervised by Professor Zhong-Qiu Zhao👍.

I was funded by the China Scholarship Council and spent a one-year exchange study at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, supervised by Prof. Xudong Jiang (IEEE Fellow) 🤞. Meanwhile, I am also lucky to have opportunities to collaborate with Henghui Ding (Professor at FDU) 🤞 and Yulun Zhang (Professor at SJTU) 🤞 .

My research focuses on low-level vision tasks, including image super-resolution, image dehazing, and image denoising🔥🔥.
